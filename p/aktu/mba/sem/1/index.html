How to Prepare Using Previous Papers

After completing your syllabus, attempt at least two to three previous year question papers for each subject. Maintain exam conditions and avoid using notes during the attempt. After solving, review your answers and identify weak areas for improvement.

Regular practice enhances conceptual clarity and improves the ability to write structured answers within the given time limit.
