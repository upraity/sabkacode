MCA vs MBA — BCA Ke Baad Kaunsa Karein?

Technical depth ya management — salary comparison aur real decision framework.

7 min read July 2024 SabkaCode Team

BCA ke baad do options saamne hain: MCA ya MBA? Dono alag paths hain, dono ke apne fayde hain. Honest comparison karte hain.

Pehle Socho: Mujhe coding pasand hai ya log manage karna? Technical depth chahiye ya business exposure?

1 MCA vs MBA — Side by Side

MCA vs MBA — dono ki apni value hai 🎓

FactorMCAMBA
Duration2 years2 years
FocusTechnical — coding, systemsManagement, Marketing, Finance
EntranceNIMCET, State testsCAT, MAT, CMAT
Starting Salary (avg)3-8 LPA3-15 LPA (college pe depend)
Growth PathDeveloper → Architect → CTOManager → Director → CEO

2 Salary Reality

Salary college quality pe heavily depend karti hai 💰

  • MCA NIT/top college: 6-15 LPA
  • MCA average college: 3-5 LPA
  • MBA IIM: 20-30 LPA starting
  • MBA average college: 2.5-4 LPA (BCA fresher jaisa)

MBA Ka Sach: Average college MBA mein sirf loan milta hai, placement nahi. Top college ya skip karo.

3 Kaunsa Choose Karein?

1

Coding pasand hai?

MCA — technical depth ke saath career banao.

2

Business/marketing mein interest?

MBA — magar CAT properly prepare karo, top college aim karo.

3

Paisa nahi higher education ke liye?

Direct job lo, skills develop karo, baad mein part-time MBA consider karo.

Bottom Line: Sirf naam ke liye degree mat lo. Jo genuinely interest ho — woh karo. Usimein success milegi.
