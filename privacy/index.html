1. Information We Collect

SabkaCode ek free educational platform hai. Hum minimal data collect karte hain — sirf wahi jo site chalane ke liye zaroori hai.

Jo hum automatically collect karte hain:

  • Browser type aur version
  • Pages visited aur time spent
  • Referring website (kahan se aaye)
  • Approximate location (country/city level only)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)

Jo hum kabhi collect NAHI karte:

  • Aapka naam ya email (jab tak aap khud email na karein)
  • Password ya login credentials (koi account nahi hai)
  • Payment ya financial information
  • Sensitive personal data

3. Cookies & Tracking

SabkaCode cookies use karta hai — yeh small text files hote hain jo aapke browser mein save hote hain aur site experience improve karte hain.

Hum in cookies ka use karte hain:

  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics — page views aur traffic track karna
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense — relevant ads dikhana
  • Preference Cookies: Aapki browser settings yaad rakhna

Aap cookies disable kar sakte ho apne browser settings se. Lekin kuch features properly kaam nahi karenge us case mein.

💡 Chrome mein cookies manage karne ke liye: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
📢

4. Google AdSense & Advertisements

SabkaCode Google AdSense use karta hai ads dikhane ke liye. Google ek third-party vendor hai jo site pe ads serve karta hai.

  • Google aapke previous visits ke basis pe personalized ads dikhata hai
  • Google DoubleClick cookie use karta hai ads ke liye
  • Aap Google ka Ads Settings visit karke personalized ads opt-out kar sakte ho
  • Ya fir www.aboutads.info pe jaake third-party vendors ka opt-out kar sakte ho

Google ki Privacy Policy yahan padh sakte ho: policies.google.com/privacy
