Our Story

2022–23 mein ek idea se shuru hua safar

SabkaCode ki neev tab rakhi gayi jab ek student ne mahsoos kiya ki notes scattered hain, previous papers dhundne mein ghante lagte hain, aur solutions milte hi nahi.

Isi problem ko solve karne ke liye SabkaCode banaya gaya — ek aisi jagah jahan BCA, MCA, MBA aur engineering ke students ko sab kuch organized mile, bilkul free.

Aaj yeh platform AKTU, CSJMU, aur DBRAU ke hazaron students ki pehli pasand ban chuka hai.
